Ingredients

  • 1 (6 oz.) ground chuck, shaped into a burger
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tsp. olive oil, divided
  • 1/2 small onion, sliced
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese or other cheese
  • 2 thick slices bacon, cut in half
  • 2 tsp. Dijon mustard
  • 1 burger bun

Instructions

  1. Brush burger lightly with 2 tsp. olive oil and grill 7 to 9 minutes; turn. Cook 5 to 7 minutes more until desired doneness.
  2. Meanwhile, toast bun and spread mustard on bottom half. Saut√© onion in remaining 1 tsp. olive oil and cook bacon until crisp.
  3. Place burger on bottom half of bun, top with onions and Cheddar.
  4. Top with tomato slice, season with salt and pepper. Top with Swiss and bacon. Close with bun top.
